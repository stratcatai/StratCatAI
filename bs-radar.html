The Radar only smells the hype. It can't tell you whether the method actually pays in today's market — that part's on you. Before you risk a dollar or an hour, get honest answers to these:

  1. Market saturation. How crowded is this exact method right now? Is everyone already doing it?
  2. Real execution difficulty. The skills, tools, and boring hidden work it actually takes — not what the guru claims.
  3. Platform risk. How likely is OpenAI / Google / Meta / TikTok to kill or nerf this in the next 6 months?
  4. Time to first dollar. Honest timeline for a normal solo person — not the highlight reel.
  5. Capital required. Real money upfront: ads, tools, accounts, subscriptions.
  6. Proof vs. hype. Independent proof it works — or just screenshots from people selling it?
  7. Sustainability. Does it compound, or die the second everyone copies it?
  8. Your edge. What do you bring that makes this work where most people quit?
